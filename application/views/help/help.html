Welcome to Lokate Student App

This page will provide HELP information regarding the product & the new UPDATES available.

Thank you for downloading the Lokate Student App. Hope you have found this app to be useful and 'easy to use'. It's our constant endeavor to improve this product thus making it easy to use & also capable. Please visit this page often to view the latest updates & news related to this product.

This app was developed as an 'aid' for parents like us to keep a tab on our dear children as they travel to/from school using school transportation.

This app is available on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.

Select school

Please select your school from the list.

The Login Page

Your login id will be your Email ID that you have provided to the school. To activate your account, you will have to SIGN-UP using the Email ID & the access code provided by the school.

Please click the SIGN UP / FORGOT PASSWORD button & follow the instructions to complete the activation process. If you still have problems, contact the school authorities.

The Menu Page

This page will provide you a host of options to know & understand your child's status & location.

MY PROFILE Page

This page will provide you your details. If you find any issues with the details mentioned, please contact the school authorities.
